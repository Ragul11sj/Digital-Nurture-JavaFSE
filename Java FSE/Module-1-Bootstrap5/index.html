Support

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before attending an event.

Simply find an event you're interested in, click the "Register" button on its card, fill in the registration form with your details, and complete the payment if applicable. You'll receive a confirmation email with your digital ticket instantly.

Refund policies vary by event organiser. Most events offer full refunds up to 7 days before the event date, and partial refunds up to 48 hours before. Check the specific event's policy on its detail page. Our support team is also available 24/7 to assist.

Many of our events are designed for all ages and include dedicated family zones, children's activities, and accessible facilities. Each event listing clearly indicates age recommendations, family-friendly features, and any restrictions.

Anyone can apply to host events on our platform. Submit your organiser application through the "Host an Event" page, complete the verification process (background check + community review), and you'll be able to list events within 5 business days.

Our mobile-optimised website works seamlessly on all devices. A dedicated iOS and Android app is currently in beta and will be publicly available in Q3 2025. Sign up for our newsletter to be the first to know when it launches.
