Community Voice

Latest News

CivicPulse has been at the heart of Chennai's community scene since 2022, connecting thousands of residents with meaningful local experiences. From cultural heritage walks along Marina Beach to cutting-edge technology workshops at TIDEL Park, we curate events that bring people together across neighbourhoods, age groups, and interests.

This quarter, we are proud to partner with over 30 local organisations to bring you a season packed with wellness retreats, culinary festivals, music nights, and hands-on skill workshops. Every event is vetted by our community panel to ensure quality, safety, and genuine neighbourhood value.

Our registration system now supports instant seat confirmation, digital passes, and event reminders via email. New this season: a loyalty programme that rewards frequent attendees with exclusive early access and discounted fees across premium workshops and festival passes.

We are actively looking for community organisers, venue partners, and volunteers for the upcoming Monsoon Fest series. If you run a local initiative that deserves a wider audience, reach out to us — CivicPulse amplifies grassroots energy and gives local talent a platform it deserves.
